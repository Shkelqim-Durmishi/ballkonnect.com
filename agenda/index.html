The Balkonnect Festival 2025 is scheduled to take place on May 23-24, 2025, at the Emerald Hotel in Prishtina, Kosovo. ​

​The Emerald Hotel is located at Magjistralja Prishtinë – Ferizaj, km. i 5-th, Prishtinë 10000, Kosovo. It is approximately a 10-minute drive from Pristina’s city center and about 15 minutes from Pristina International Airport.

The hotel offers luxurious accommodations with spacious rooms equipped with modern amenities. Guests can enjoy facilities such as two restaurants serving a variety of cuisines, free Wi-Fi, and 24-hour room service. Its strategic location and comprehensive services make it a preferred choice for both business and leisure travelers visiting Pristina.​

Participants are entitled to a 20% discount on accommodation at the Emerald Hotel. To avail of this offer, please send your reservation request to  [email protected], mentioning that you are attending the Balkonnect Festival 2025.
