Continuing the momentum from Day 1, Day 2 of Balkonnect 2024 on May 24 brings specialized sessions aimed at tech enthusiasts, social innovators, and investors. Check out the detailed schedule for a day packed with high-impact discussions:

10:00–11:30

ICT & Digital
Delve into the latest digital trends, innovations in ICT, and opportunities for startups in the tech space. Learn from experts on how the digital economy is shaping the future.

10:00–11:30

Social Enterprise & Women Entrepreneurship
This session celebrates social impact businesses and women in entrepreneurship. Get inspired by successful case studies and discover how you can make a difference while scaling your business.

11:30–13:00

Outsourcing
A session for businesses exploring outsourcing as a growth strategy. Understand the opportunities for outsourcing across industries and how it can help your business scale efficiently.

13:00–15:00

One-on-One Investor Meetings
Pre-scheduled one-on-one meetings between investors and startups. This is your chance to meet potential investors and pitch your business directly to the right audience.

14:00–15:30

Banking & Finance
Access to capital is key to growing your business. This session brings together experts in banking, finance, and fintech to discuss investment strategies, trends, and funding opportunities.

21:00–23:00

Private Investors, BAs & VCs Pitching Night
This is your chance to pitch your business directly to private investors, business angels, and venture capitalists. Don’t miss out on this exclusive opportunity to secure investment!

B2B Sessions

11:00–18:00

Like Day 1, B2B meetings will run parallel to the main program, giving you the chance to connect and create valuable partnerships.

The participants should benefit from this experience in that, it will give them new ideas and widening their business perspective and vision. They could also explore the possibility of international business cooperation.

Balkonnect Festival 2025

The Premier Business, Innovation & Investment Event in the Western Balkans

Prishtina, Kosovo | May 23-24, 2025

The Balkonnect Festival 2025, set to take place in Prishtina, Kosovo, on May 23-24, is the ultimate platform for fostering business connections, investment opportunities and innovation in the Western Balkans. The festival brings together industry leaders, investors, entrepreneurs and policymakers from across the region and beyond to engage in sectorial B2B matchmaking, thematic panel discussions and high-impact pitching Sessions.

A Business Festival with Global Impact

Organized by Prishtina REA, in partnership with international organizations such as EBRD, EEN, and EBN, the event aims to accelerate cross-regional economic growth by showcasing cutting-edge innovation, strategic partnerships and investment-ready businesses.

Balkonnect Festival 2025 is not just a conference-it is a dynamic business marketplace, offering:

  • Non-stop sectorial B2B matchmaking - Helping businesses connect with potential partners, clients, and investors
  • High-profile sectorial thematic panels - Showcasing industry trends, exceptional success stories, and innovation across key sectors.
  • The Balkonnect Pitching Event - Providing startups and scale-ups with access to investors and financing/funding opportunities
  • The Balkonnect Pitching Networking at its best - Enabling structured and informal engagements to create lasting business linkage’s and collaborations.Event - Providing startups and scale-ups with access to investors and financing/funding opportunities
